Elite Units

Marine Force Recon

Marine Force Recon is a special operations force within the United States Marine Corps, specializing in reconnaissance and direct action missions. They are known for their rigorous training and ability to operate in diverse environments.

MARSOC Marine Raiders

MARSOC (Marine Special Operations Command) Marine Raiders are an elite special operations unit of the Marine Corps, focusing on unconventional warfare, direct action, and special reconnaissance. They are known for their versatility and ability to operate in small teams.

Marine Martime Raid Force (MRF)

Marine Corps Security Forces

Marine Corps Security Forces are responsible for providing security and protection for naval installations, ships, and personnel. They are trained in various security operations and are often deployed to high-risk areas.

Fleet Anti-Terrorism Security Team

The Fleet Anti-terrorism Security Team (FAST) is an elite United States Marine Corps unit specializing in rapid-response security missions, high-risk asset defense, and counter-terrorism operations, deployed worldwide to protect US interests.
